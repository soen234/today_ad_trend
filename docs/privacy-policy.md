# Privacy Policy for TAT (Today Ad Trend)

**Last Updated: December 29, 2025**

## Introduction

TAT ("Today Ad Trend", "we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

## Information We Collect

### Information Collected Automatically

When you use our app, we may automatically collect:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: App interactions, features used, time spent in the app
- **Advertising Data**: Ad impressions, clicks, and interactions

### Third-Party Data Collection

Our app uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. Google may collect:

- Advertising ID (IDFA on iOS, GAID on Android)
- IP address
- Device information
- Ad interaction data

For more information about Google's data practices, please visit: https://policies.google.com/privacy

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Display relevant advertisements
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Analyze app usage patterns
- Comply with legal obligations

## Third-Party Services

### Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to serve advertisements. AdMob may use cookies and similar technologies to collect data for personalized advertising. You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings:

- **iOS**: Settings > Privacy > Tracking
- **Android**: Settings > Google > Ads > Opt out of Ads Personalization

## Data Retention

We do not store personal data on our servers. Any data collected through third-party services (Google AdMob) is subject to their respective retention policies.

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Opt out of personalized advertising
- Request information about data collection
- Delete your advertising ID by resetting it in device settings

## Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy in the app.
