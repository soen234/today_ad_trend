üöÄ Getting Started

Learn how to install and use the TAT app.

    Download the App

    Search for 'TAT' or 'Today Ad Trend' on the App Store or Google Play to download the app.

  • 2

    Launch the App

  • 3

    Explore Tabs

    Use the bottom tab bar to navigate between Banner, Interstitial, Rewarded, and Settings screens.

üì± Using Banner Ads

Banner ads are rectangular ads that occupy part of the app screen. In TAT, you can view various banner sizes.

Banner Type Size Features
Banner 320x50 Standard mobile banner
Large Banner 320x100 Greater visibility
Medium Rectangle 300x250 Ideal between content
Adaptive Auto Fits screen width

üí° Tip

Use the filter buttons to view only specific banner types. Select 'All' to see all types at once.

üì∫ Using Interstitial Ads

Interstitial ads are full-screen ads that cover the entire screen. They typically appear between game levels or at content transition points.

  • 1

    Select Interstitial Tab

    Tap the Interstitial icon in the bottom tab bar.

  • 2

    Wait for Ad Loading

    Wait until the Status changes to 'Ready'. While the ad is loading, it will show 'Loading...'.

  • 3

    View the Ad

    Tap the 'Show Ad' button to view the interstitial ad. A close button will appear after a certain time.

‚ö†Ô∏è Note

Interstitial ads can disrupt user experience, so they should be used carefully at appropriate times in real apps.

üéÅ Using Rewarded Ads

Rewarded ads are ads that users voluntarily watch in exchange for rewards. This is the ad format with the highest user satisfaction.

  • 1

    Select Rewarded Tab

    Select the Rewarded (gift icon) tab from the bottom tab bar.

  • 2

    Watch the Ad

    Tap the 'Watch Ad & Earn' button to watch the rewarded ad. You must watch the entire ad to receive the reward.

  • 3

    Check Rewards

    After watching the ad, the 'Total Rewards Earned' counter will increase. You'll also see a notification confirming your reward.

üí° Benefits of Rewarded Ads

Rewarded ads have the highest eCPM and minimize negative experiences because users choose to watch them.

üì∏ Screenshot Save Feature

You can save ads you like to your gallery from the Banner ads screen. This is useful for collecting ad references.

  • 1

    Find the Save Button

    There's a blue 'Save' button at the bottom of each banner ad card.

  • 2

    Grant Permission

    The first time you save, you'll be asked for gallery access permission. Select 'Allow'.

  • 3

    Save Complete

    A notification will appear when saved successfully. Check the saved image in your gallery.

‚ùì FAQ

  • Q

    Ads won't load

    Check your internet connection. Make sure Wi-Fi or mobile data is enabled. Try restarting the app.

  • Q

    I didn't receive my reward

    You must watch the rewarded ad until the end to receive the reward. If you close it mid-way, you won't receive the reward.

  • Q

    Can't save screenshots

    Check if gallery/photo access permission is granted in your device settings.
